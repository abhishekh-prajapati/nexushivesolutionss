FOUNDER'S INTELLIGENCE

Expert Tips for New Founders

Critical operational nuances often overlooked during the rapid scaling phase.

The 2-Day Buffer Rule

Always aim to file at least 2 days before the deadline. Government portals (GSTN/PF) often face heavy traffic and downtime on due dates.

Penalty Warning

Late PF/ESI payments attract 12% interest plus damages up to 25%. These are non-deductible expenses in your taxes—affecting your bottom line directly.

State Nuances

Professional Tax and LWF dates shift depending on where your office is registered. Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Tamil Nadu have distinct statutory timelines.

TERMINOLOGY

The Founder's Glossary

Decoding the complex language of HR, Law, and Compliance.

CTC (Cost to Company)
The total salary package including all benefits, bonuses, and statutory contributions from the employer's side.
Statutory Audit
A legally required review of the accuracy of a company's financial statements and records.
Internal Committee (IC)
The mandatory body required under the POSH Act to handle complaints of sexual harassment in the workplace.
ESI (Employee State Insurance)
A self-financing social security and health insurance scheme for Indian workers.
Professional Tax (PT)
A tax levied by various state governments on salaried individuals and professionals.
LWF (Labour Welfare Fund)
A state-managed fund for providing social security and improving working conditions for laborers.
